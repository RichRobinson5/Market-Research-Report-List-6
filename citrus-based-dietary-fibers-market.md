<p><p>Citrus based dietary fibers can be categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Pectin is a soluble fiber found in citrus fruits that can help regulate digestion and boost immunity. Hemicellulose is a complex carbohydrate that provides bulk and aids in regular bowel movements. Cellulose is a non-soluble fiber that supports digestive health and helps in maintaining a healthy weight. These different types of citrus based fibers offer various benefits for overall health and well-being.</p></p>
