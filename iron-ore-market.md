<p><ul><li>Sinter feed</li><li>Pellet feed</li><li>Lump</li><li>Pellet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron ore market comprises various product types tailored for steel production. Sinter feed is fine ore used in sinter plants, which agglomerate it into larger pieces. Pellet feed consists of even finer ore processed into iron pellets for blast furnaces. Lump ore is naturally occurring, larger chunks that can be directly charged into a furnace. The pellet market emphasizes the sale of processed pellets, prized for their high iron content and efficiency in reducing emissions during steelmaking.</p></p>
