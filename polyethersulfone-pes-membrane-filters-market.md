<p><p>Polyethersulfone (PES) membrane filters are categorized into two types based on pore size - those with pore size less than or equal to 0.5µm and those with pore size greater than 0.5µm. PES membrane filters with pore size <= 0.5µm are commonly used for the filtration of small particles and microorganisms in pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and food and beverage industries. On the other hand, PES membrane filters with pore size > 0.5µm are typically utilized for larger particle filtration in applications such as water treatment and chemical processing.</p></p>
