<p><p>Semiconductor testing boards play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices. The three main types of testing boards are ProbeCard, LoadBoard, and Burn-inBoard. ProbeCards are used for testing the electrical performance of the semiconductor devices. LoadBoards are designed to test the communication and power functions. Burn-inBoards are specifically used to stress-test semiconductor devices by subjecting them to extreme conditions for extended periods. Each type of testing board serves a specific purpose in the semiconductor industry to ensure the overall quality of the devices.</p></p>
