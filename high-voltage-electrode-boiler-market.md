<p><p>High Voltage Electrode Boilers are classified into three main types based on their electrode configuration. Fully Submerged Electrode Boilers have all electrodes fully immersed in water, providing efficient heat transfer. Semi-immersed Electrode Boilers have some electrodes partially submerged, offering flexibility in operation. Jet Electrode Boilers utilize a jet design for electrode water immersion, maximizing efficiency. Each type caters to different heating requirements and applications, providing options for industries seeking high voltage electrode boiler solutions.</p></p>
