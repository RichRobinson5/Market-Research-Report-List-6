<p><p>Direct-draw draft beer systems are typically used in smaller establishments where the kegs are located directly beneath the tap. Long-draw systems are used in larger establishments where the kegs are located further away from the tap, requiring a longer system of tubing. Air cooled systems use a fan to cool the beer lines as opposed to glycol-cooled systems which use a separate cooling unit. Each type of draft beer system caters to different establishment sizes and layouts, ensuring that the beer is served at the optimal temperature and quality.</p></p>
