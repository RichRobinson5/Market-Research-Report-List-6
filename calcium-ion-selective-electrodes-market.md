<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calcium ion selective electrodes (ISEs) are essential tools for measuring calcium levels in various applications, and they primarily fall into two market types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a hydrophobic organic solvent containing specific ionophores, allowing selective calcium ion detection. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes employ a matrix of polyvinyl chloride, often doped with ion-selective additives, to achieve similar selectivity. Both types are crucial in fields like agriculture, environmental monitoring, and clinical diagnostics for accurate calcium measurement.</p></p>
