<p><p>The Electric Power Steering System Motor market consists of different types of motors - CEPS Motor, REPS Motor, PEPS Motor, and EHPS Motor. CEPS Motor (Column Electric Power Steering) is mounted on the steering column, REPS Motor (Rack Electric Power Steering) is mounted on the steering rack, PEPS Motor (Pinion Electric Power Steering) is mounted on the steering pinion, and EHPS Motor (Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering) combines electric and hydraulic systems for enhanced performance. Each type of motor caters to specific requirements and applications within the electric power steering system.</p></p>
