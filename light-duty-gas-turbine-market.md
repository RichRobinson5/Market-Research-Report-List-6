<p><p>The Light Duty Gas Turbine market can be classified into two types based on the arrangement of the shafts: Single-axis and Two-axis configurations. In single-axis configuration, the compressor and power turbine share a single shaft, resulting in a simpler design with lower efficiency but less cost. On the other hand, in two-axis configuration, the compressor and power turbine have separate shafts, allowing for higher efficiency and flexibility in operation, but at a higher cost. These two types cater to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
