<p><p>Semiconductor strain gauge sensors come in two main market types: naked gages and backed gages. Naked gages are typically used in applications where direct contact with the surface being measured is necessary, allowing for precise readings. On the other hand, backed gages are equipped with a backing material that provides additional support and protection, making them more durable and suitable for harsh environments. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries for measuring and monitoring strain and deformation in materials and structures.</p></p>
