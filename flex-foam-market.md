<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flex Foam is available in two main market types: closed-cell and open-cell. Closed-cell foam has tightly packed cells that are filled with gas, making it firm and rigid. This type of foam is ideal for applications requiring high durability and insulation properties. On the other hand, open-cell foam has interconnected cells that allow air to pass through, giving it a softer and more flexible texture. Open-cell foam is commonly used for cushioning and sound absorption applications. Both market types provide unique benefits and are suitable for different purposes.</p></p>
