<p><p>PET shrink film is available in three main types based on their shrinkage properties: Low Shrink Film, Medium Shrink Film, and High Shrink Film. Low Shrink Film offers minimal shrinkage, making it suitable for packaging products that are sensitive to heat. Medium Shrink Film provides moderate shrinkage for a wide range of products. High Shrink Film offers maximum shrinkage, ideal for tightly wrapping irregularly shaped items. Each type caters to different packaging needs and requirements, providing flexibility and versatility in the market.</p></p>
