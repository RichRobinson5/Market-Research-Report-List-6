<p><p>Amorphous silicon and vanadium oxide are two types of uncooled thermal modules used in various industries. Amorphous silicon modules are known for their low-cost manufacturing process and high sensitivity to temperature changes, making them ideal for applications requiring high thermal resolution. Vanadium oxide modules, on the other hand, offer better stability and reliability in extreme conditions, such as high temperatures or vibrations. Both types of modules play a crucial role in thermal imaging systems, surveillance cameras, and industrial monitoring equipment.</p></p>
