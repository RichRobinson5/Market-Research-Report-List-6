<p><p>Software-Defined WAN solutions come in three main market types: Virtual Appliance, Physical Appliance, and Hybrid. Virtual Appliance solutions are software-based and run on standard hardware, providing flexibility and scalability. Physical Appliance solutions are hardware-based devices that are purpose-built for SD-WAN functionality, offering high performance and reliability. Hybrid solutions combine elements of both virtual and physical appliances, allowing organizations to tailor their SD-WAN deployment to meet specific requirements and budget constraints. Each market type offers unique benefits and features to suit different business needs.</p></p>
