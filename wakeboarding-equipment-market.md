<p><p>The wakeboarding equipment market can be categorized based on rocker types: continuous, hybrid, three-stage, and five-stage. Continuous rocker boards have a smooth curve for stability and predictable rides. Hybrid rocker boards offer a blend of smoothness and agility for versatility. Three-stage rocker boards have a more pronounced curve for aggressive pop off the wake. Five-stage rocker boards have multiple angles for maximum speed and control. Each type caters to different skill levels and riding styles in the wakeboarding industry.</p></p>
