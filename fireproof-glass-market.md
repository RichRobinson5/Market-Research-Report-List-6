<p><strong>The Fireproof Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired</li><li>Ceramic</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Tempered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fireproof glass is available in various types, including wired, ceramic, laminated, and tempered. Wired glass has a layer of wire mesh embedded within it for added strength and fire resistance. Ceramic glass is made by applying a ceramic coating to the glass surface, enhancing its fireproofing qualities. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers of glass bonded together with a fire-resistant interlayer. Tempered glass is heat-treated to increase its strength and resistance to high temperatures. Each type offers unique advantages in different fireproofing applications.</p></p>
