<p><p>Multilayer cast film lines are used for manufacturing various types of films, including CPP (cast polypropylene) film line and CPE (cast polyethylene) film line. CPP film lines are commonly used for packaging applications due to their superior clarity, heat resistance, and moisture barrier properties. On the other hand, CPE film lines are known for their flexibility, toughness, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications such as food packaging, medical devices, and industrial products. Both types of film lines are essential in the packaging industry for different specific requirements.</p></p>
