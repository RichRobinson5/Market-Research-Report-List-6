<p><p>Injection Stretch Blow Molding Machines are categorized into two types - Single-Stage Type and Two-Stage Type. Single-Stage Type machines combine the injection molding and stretch blow molding processes into a single machine, making them more efficient and cost-effective for producing PET containers. On the other hand, Two-Stage Type machines involve separate injection molding and stretch blow molding machines, allowing for more flexibility in production and the ability to produce a wider range of container sizes and shapes.</p></p>
