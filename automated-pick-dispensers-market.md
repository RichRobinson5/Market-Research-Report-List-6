<p><p>Automated Pick Dispensers market offers various types of systems, including Pusher Sorting System, Carbel Sorting, Line Shaft Diverter, and Swing Arm Diverter. Pusher Sorting System uses pusher arms to propel products towards the picking area. Carbel Sorting system uses rotating belts to move items to the desired location. Line Shaft Diverter utilizes a centralized shaft to divert products efficiently. Swing Arm Diverter uses pivoting arms to guide items in different directions. Each type of system offers unique advantages for automated picking and dispensing operations.</p></p>
