<p><p>Crystal ovens are classified into two main market types: open and sealed. Open crystal ovens feature a design where the crystal is not enclosed, allowing for easier access and maintenance. On the other hand, sealed crystal ovens have a protective casing that prevents contaminants from affecting the crystal's performance. Sealed ovens are preferred in applications requiring greater precision and stability, while open ovens are suitable for less stringent environments where easy access is prioritized. Both types cater to varying needs in the market for crystal ovens.</p></p>
