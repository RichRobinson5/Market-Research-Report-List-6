<p><p>ADSS cables are commonly found in two main market types: ADSS Flextube and ADSS Loose Tube. ADSS Flextube cables have a unique design that enables them to easily conform to the poles or towers they are mounted on, making them ideal for shorter spans and distribution networks. On the other hand, ADSS Loose Tube cables are better suited for longer span lengths and higher tension applications, as they have a traditional loose tube construction that provides extra protection for the fibers. Both types offer reliable and efficient solutions for aerial fiber optic networks.</p></p>
