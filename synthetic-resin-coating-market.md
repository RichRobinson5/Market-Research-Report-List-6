<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Water Type</li><li>Solvent-free Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic resin coatings are classified into three types based on their composition: solvent type, water type, and solvent-free type. Solvent type coatings use organic solvents as carriers for the resin, water type coatings use water as the carrier, and solvent-free coatings do not require any carrier solvent. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, application, and environmental impact. The choice of coating type depends on the specific requirements of the application and desired characteristics of the final coating.</p></p>
