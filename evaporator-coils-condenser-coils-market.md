<p><p>Evaporator coils and condenser coils are essential components of refrigeration and air conditioning systems. There are three main types of coils - tubed, finned/plated, and combined. Tubed coils consist of a series of tubes through which the refrigerant flows. Finned/plated coils have fins or plates attached to the tubes to increase surface area for better heat transfer. Combined coils incorporate both tubed and finned/plated designs for optimal efficiency. Each type of coil offers unique advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
