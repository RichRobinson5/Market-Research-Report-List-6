<p><p>Pumped Hydro Storage Market includes Closed-loop System, Open-loop System, and Other markets. Closed-loop System involves two connected reservoirs with a reversible pump turbine to move water between them, providing energy storage. Open-loop System uses natural bodies of water as the lower reservoir, utilizing a dam or canal to manipulate water levels for energy storage. Other market types may include innovative variations or combinations of these systems to meet specific energy storage needs, such as underground or offshore pumped hydro storage systems.</p></p>
