<p><p>Telescopic Sight market consists of different types including Collimating Optical Sight and Reflex Sight. Collimating Optical Sight, also known as holographic sights, use a reticle pattern to create a virtual image of the target. These sights are often used for shooting sports and hunting. Reflex Sights, on the other hand, use a single aiming point for quick target acquisition. They are popular among military and law enforcement personnel for their speed and accuracy. Both types of sights offer improved accuracy and target acquisition for shooters.</p></p>
