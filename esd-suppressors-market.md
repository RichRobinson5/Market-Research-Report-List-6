<p><p>ESD suppressors protect electronic devices from electrostatic discharge. The market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Diode Array**: Combines multiple diodes in a single package to absorb ESD events effectively. </p><p>2. **Polymer**: Utilizes conductive polymers for protection, offering fast response times and compact designs. </p><p>3. **TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressors)**: Specialized diodes that clamp voltage spikes, providing robust defense against transients. </p><p>4. **Zener**: Zener diodes regulate voltage levels and can also mitigate ESD, ensuring circuit stability. These types cater to diverse applications in electronics.</p></p>
