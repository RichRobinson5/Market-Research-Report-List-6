<p><p>The zeolite for detergents market primarily consists of four types: Zeolite A, Zeolite P, Zeolite X, and Zeolite AX. Zeolite A is widely utilized for its high efficiency in water softening, making it popular in laundry detergents. Zeolite P offers excellent adsorption properties, enhancing stain removal. Zeolite X is known for its ability to retain stability at elevated temperatures, catering to high-performance formulations. Lastly, Zeolite AX combines characteristics of both A and X types, offering versatile applications in cleaning products.</p></p>
