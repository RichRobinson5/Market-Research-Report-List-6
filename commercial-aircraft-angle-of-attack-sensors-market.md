<p><ul><li>Vane angle of attack sensor</li><li>Zero pressure angle of attack sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aircraft angle of attack sensors market includes two main types: vane angle of attack sensors and zero pressure angle of attack sensors. Vane sensors use a mechanical vane to measure the angle at which the aircraft is flying relative to the oncoming air. Zero pressure sensors rely on pressure differences between the top and bottom of a small pitot tube to determine the angle of attack. Both types of sensors are crucial for ensuring the safety and stability of commercial aircraft during flight.</p></p>
