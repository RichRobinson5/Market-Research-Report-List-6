<p><p>Common Mode Choke Coils (CMCC) are passive electrical components used to suppress high-frequency noise in electronic circuits by blocking common mode signals while allowing differential signals to pass through. The market for CMCC is divided into two main types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Common Mode Choke Coils, which are compact and easily integrated into modern PCB designs, and Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils, which are typically larger and offer enhanced durability for various applications in industrial and consumer electronics.</p></p>
