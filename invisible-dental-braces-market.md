<p><p>Invisible dental braces come in two main types - lingual braces, also known as behind the teeth braces, and external braces, which are placed outside the teeth. Lingual braces are attached to the back of the teeth, making them virtually invisible from the front. External braces, on the other hand, are attached to the front of the teeth but are designed to blend in with the natural color of the teeth, making them less noticeable. Both types offer a discreet alternative to traditional metal braces.</p></p>
