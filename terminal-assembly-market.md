<p><p>Terminal assembly market consists of various types such as PCB mounted terminal assembly, barrier terminal assembly, and straight-through terminal assembly. PCB mounted terminal assembly is directly mounted on printed circuit boards for electrical connections. Barrier terminal assembly features insulated barriers to prevent short circuits. Straight-through terminal assembly is used for straight wire connections without any barriers. Each type caters to different needs and requirements based on the specific applications and environments in which they are used.</p></p>
