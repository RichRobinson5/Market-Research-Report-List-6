<p><p>Nanofabrication market includes two main types - Traditional Processing and Non-traditional Processing. Traditional Processing involves conventional methods like lithography, etching, and deposition to create nanostructures. On the other hand, Non-traditional Processing uses new techniques such as nanoimprint lithography, chemical vapor deposition, and molecular self-assembly. Both types play a crucial role in the development of nanotechnology, with traditional methods offering reliability and precision, while non-traditional methods offer innovative and cost-effective solutions for producing nanoscale devices and structures.</p></p>
