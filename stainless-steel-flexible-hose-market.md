<p><p>Stainless steel flexible hoses are widely used in various industries for their durability and versatility. There are two main types of stainless steel flexible hoses in the market: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses are constructed in a helical strip pattern, providing flexibility and strength. Corrugated hoses have a series of convolutions or folds, allowing for increased flexibility and better pressure resistance. Both types of hoses are popular choices for applications requiring high pressure and temperature resistance.</p></p>
