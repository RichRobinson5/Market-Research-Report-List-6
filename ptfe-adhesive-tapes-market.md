<p><p>PTFE adhesive tapes are available in a variety of densities to suit different applications. Low density tapes have a lower fluoropolymer content, making them more flexible for conforming to irregular surfaces. Standard density tapes offer a good balance of flexibility and durability for general use. Medium density tapes provide increased chemical resistance and heat resistance. High density tapes have the highest level of durability and abrasion resistance, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Each density type caters to specific needs and requirements within the market.</p></p>
