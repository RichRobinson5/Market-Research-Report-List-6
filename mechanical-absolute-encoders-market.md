<p><ul><li>Singleturn Encoder</li><li>Multiturn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical Absolute Encoders are available in two main types - Singleturn Encoder and Multiturn Encoder. Singleturn Encoders output a unique digital code for each position within one revolution, offering high precision in tasks that require exact position detection. Multiturn Encoders, on the other hand, provide multiple revolutions of position data in addition to the singleturn functionality, making them ideal for applications where extensive rotation needs to be tracked accurately. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, including manufacturing and robotics.</p></p>
