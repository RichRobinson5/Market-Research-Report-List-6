<p><p>The riser cleaning tool market can be categorized into three main types: Riser Magnet, Brush, and Other tools. Riser Magnets are used to attract and remove ferrous debris from risers, ensuring smooth flow and preventing blockages. Brush tools are designed to scrub and clean the inner walls of risers, removing any buildup or residue. Other tools in the market may include scrapers, vacuums, or air blasts to effectively clean risers and maintain optimal performance. Each type of tool offers unique benefits for keeping risers clean and efficient.</p></p>
