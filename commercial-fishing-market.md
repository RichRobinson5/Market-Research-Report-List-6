<p><p>Commercial fishing markets can be divided into different types based on the methods used to catch fish. Longliners use long lines with baited hooks to catch specific species of fish. Dredgers use a type of fishing gear known as a dredge, which is dragged along the sea floor to catch shellfish and other bottom-dwelling species. Each market type has its own unique approach to harvesting seafood for commercial purposes.</p></p>
