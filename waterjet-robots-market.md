<p><p>The Waterjet Robots market consists of three main types: Continuous Jet, Pulse Jet, and Cavitation Jet. Continuous Jet waterjets provide a steady stream of high-pressure water for cutting materials. Pulse Jet waterjets deliver water in pulses, allowing for more precise control and versatility in cutting. Cavitation Jet waterjets use cavitation bubbles to enhance cutting power, making them suitable for tougher materials. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
