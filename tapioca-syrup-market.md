<p><strong>The Tapioca Syrup Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tapioca Syrup</li><li>Tapioca Maltodextrins</li><li>Tapioca Syrup Solids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tapioca syrup is a natural sweetener made from tapioca starch through a process of hydrolysis, resulting in a liquid form of glucose. Tapioca maltodextrins are derived from tapioca syrup through further processing, resulting in a powdered form of glucose. Tapioca syrup solids are formed by dehydrating tapioca syrup to remove the water content. These products are commonly used as sweeteners and thickeners in food and beverage industries, offering an alternative to traditional sugar sources.</p></p>
