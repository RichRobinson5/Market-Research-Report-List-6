<p><ul><li>Liquid Conductive Gel</li><li>Spray Conductive Gel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG Gel market comprises two main types: Liquid Conductive Gel and Spray Conductive Gel. Liquid Conductive Gel is a gel-like substance that is applied directly to the skin before attaching the ECG electrodes, ensuring a good electrical connection and accurate readings. Spray Conductive Gel, on the other hand, is applied through a spray bottle, providing a convenient and mess-free alternative to traditional gel application. Both types are essential in performing electrocardiograms effectively and are widely used in medical settings.</p></p>
