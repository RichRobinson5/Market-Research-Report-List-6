<p><strong>The Acrylic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Acrylic Resin</li><li>Thermoplastic Acrylic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic resin market consists of two main types: thermosetting acrylic resin and thermoplastic acrylic resin. Thermosetting acrylic resins, once cured by heat or chemical reaction, become rigid and can't be melted or reshaped. They are used in applications requiring high heat resistance and durability. On the other hand, thermoplastic acrylic resins can be melted and reshaped multiple times, making them suitable for applications where flexibility and ease of processing are important. Both types offer unique properties and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
