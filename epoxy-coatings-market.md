<p><ul><li>Solvent borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powder-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy coatings are available in three main types: solvent borne, waterborne, and powder-based. Solvent borne epoxy coatings contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and require solvents for application. Waterborne epoxy coatings on the other hand use water as a carrier, making them more environmentally friendly. Powder-based epoxy coatings are applied by electrostatic spray deposition and then cured in an oven. Each type has its own set of benefits and applications, catering to different industries and preferences in the market.</p></p>
