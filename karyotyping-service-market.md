<p><p>Karyotyping service market includes various types such as G-banding analysis, whole genome sequencing analysis, and others. In G-banding analysis, the chromosomes are stained to identify any structural abnormalities. Whole genome sequencing analysis involves sequencing an individual's entire genetic code to detect any mutations or genetic disorders. Other services in the market may include FISH analysis, spectral karyotyping, and comparative genomic hybridization. These different types of analyses provide valuable insights into a person's genetic makeup and help in diagnosing various genetic disorders.</p></p>
