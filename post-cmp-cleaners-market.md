<p><ul><li>Acidic Post CMP Cleaners</li><li>Alkaline Post CMP Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post CMP cleaners are used in the semiconductor industry to remove residues left on the wafer surface after chemical mechanical planarization (CMP). There are two main types of post CMP cleaners in the market: acidic and alkaline.</p><p>Acidic post CMP cleaners are formulated with acids such as hydrochloric acid or sulfuric acid to effectively remove metal oxides and organic contaminants from the wafer surface. </p><p>Alkaline post CMP cleaners, on the other hand, use alkaline solutions like ammonia or potassium hydroxide to clean the wafer surface by neutralizing acids and dissolving organic residues.</p></p>
