<p><strong>The Precision Investment Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sodium Silicate Process</li><li>Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/ Silica Sol Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision Investment Castings are categorized into two main types: the Sodium Silicate Process and the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol Process. </p><p>The Sodium Silicate Process involves using a water glass and refractory coating to create precision castings. On the other hand, the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol Process relies on a ceramic shell made from a silica sol and stearic acid mix. Both processes offer high precision and accuracy in casting metal parts, making them popular choices in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
