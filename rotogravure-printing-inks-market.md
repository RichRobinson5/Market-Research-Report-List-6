Nitrocellulose inks are fast drying and offer high gloss finish, while Polyamide inks are known for their excellent adhesion and abrasion resistance properties. Polyurethane inks are highly versatile and provide good chemical resistance, whereas Acrylic inks are environmentally friendly and offer good lightfastness. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different printing requirements and substrates in the market.</p></p>
