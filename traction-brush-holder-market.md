<p><p>Traction brush holders are key components in various industrial applications, coming in three main types: Pocket Holder, Pocket Radial Holder, and Pocket Reaction Holder. The Pocket Holder is ideal for applications requiring constant force, while the Pocket Radial Holder is designed for radial traction. The Pocket Reaction Holder is tailored for applications needing both radial and axial traction. These holders serve different purposes in maintaining brush contact with rotating surfaces, ensuring efficient operation and performance in a variety of industrial settings.</p></p>
