<p><p>Biologic excipients market includes various types such as Polymers, Sugar Alcohols, Polysorbates, Inorganic Salts, Amino Acids, Surfactants, and others. Polymers are used as stabilizers, gelling agents, and viscosity enhancers. Sugar alcohols act as sweeteners and bulking agents. Polysorbates are used as emulsifiers and surfactants in biologic formulations. Inorganic salts help maintain osmolarity and pH balance. Amino acids serve as stabilizers and buffering agents. Surfactants aid in solubilization and dispersion. Other excipients include antioxidants, preservatives, and flavoring agents.</p></p>
