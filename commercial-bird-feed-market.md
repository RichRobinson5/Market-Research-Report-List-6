<p><p>The commercial bird feed market can be categorized into staple and supplement types. Staple bird feeds serve as the primary diet for birds, providing essential nutrients and energy for optimal health. These often include seeds, grains, and pellets. In contrast, supplement feeds are designed to enhance the diet, offering additional nutrients, vitamins, or minerals that may be lacking in staple feeds. Supplements can come in the form of treats, fortified mixtures, or specialized feeds tailored to specific bird species' needs.</p></p>
