<p><p>A Video Mediastinoscope is a specialized medical device used for minimally invasive procedures to examine the mediastinum, which is the area in the chest between the lungs. The rising prevalence of lung cancer and other respiratory diseases, along with increasing demand for minimally invasive procedures, are driving the growth of the market. Technological advancements in endoscopic imaging and surgical techniques, as well as the availability of advanced video mediastinoscopes with better visualization and improved ergonomics, are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing adoption of video-assisted thoracic surgery (VATS) for diagnosing and treating mediastinal disorders is expected to further propel the market.
