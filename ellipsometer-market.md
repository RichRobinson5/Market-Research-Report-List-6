<p><ul><li>Spectroscopic Ellipsometer</li><li>Laser Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectroscopic ellipsometer is a type of ellipsometer that measures the change in polarization state of light reflected from a sample across a range of wavelengths, providing detailed information about the optical properties of the sample. On the other hand, laser ellipsometer utilizes laser light as a source for measuring the phase change of polarized light reflected from a sample surface, offering high precision and accuracy in analyzing thin film layers. Both types are widely used in various industries for thin film characterization and material analysis.</p></p>
