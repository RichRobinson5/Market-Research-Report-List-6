<p><ul><li>Swirl Type Pulverized Coal Burner</li><li>Once-Through Pulverized Coal Burner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swirl Type Pulverized Coal Burner is a type of burner where the combustion air and pulverized coal are mixed in a swirling motion before being fed into the furnace, resulting in a more stable and efficient combustion process. On the other hand, Once-Through Pulverized Coal Burner is a type of burner where the combustion air and pulverized coal are fed directly into the furnace in a straight line, allowing for higher air and fuel mixing rates and better flame stability. Both types are commonly used in industrial boilers and power plants for efficient coal combustion.</p></p>
