<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wrap film market is primarily divided into two types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is produced by casting molten resin onto a flat surface, resulting in a thinner, more flexible, and durable product ideal for intricate curves and contours of vehicles. In contrast, calendered film is made by passing resin through rollers, producing a thicker and more economical option suited for flat surfaces and larger areas. Each type offers distinct advantages, influencing choice based on vehicle design and application needs.</p></p>
