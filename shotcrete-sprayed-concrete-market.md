<p><p>Shotcrete-sprayed concrete is used in various applications such as construction, mining, tunneling, and repair work. There are two main types of shotcrete-sprayed concrete market: wet mix and dry mix. Wet mix shotcrete involves mixing all ingredients before spraying, resulting in a smoother finish and less rebound. Dry mix shotcrete involves mixing dry ingredients at the nozzle and is preferred for its convenience and lower rebound. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific project requirements.</p></p>
