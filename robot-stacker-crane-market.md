<p><p>Robot stacker cranes are commonly categorized into two main types based on their structure: Monocolumn Stacker Cranes and Two-column Stacker Cranes. Monocolumn stacker cranes feature a single upright column to support the crane's movement and lifting capabilities. On the other hand, two-column stacker cranes have two vertical columns for extra stability and support, allowing them to handle heavier loads and operate more efficiently. Both types of stacker cranes serve the same purpose of automating the stacking and retrieval of goods in warehouses or production facilities.</p></p>
