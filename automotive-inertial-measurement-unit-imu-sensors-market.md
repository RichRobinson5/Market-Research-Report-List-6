<p><strong>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors are essential components in vehicles for accurately measuring their orientation, velocity, and gravitational forces. There are two main types of IMUs in the market: MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs which use micro-electromechanical systems technology for precise measurements, and Fiber Optic Gyroscope (FOG)-based IMUs which rely on the interference of light to determine orientation. MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs are generally more compact and cost-effective, while FOG-based IMUs offer higher accuracy and reliability but at a higher price point.</p></p>
