<p><p>The Flatwire HAIR-PIN Motor market features three main winding types that enhance motor efficiency and performance. Hairpin winding utilizes hairpin-shaped conductors to improve space utilization and reduce losses. Double-ended welding winding involves connecting two ends of a wire, facilitating simpler assembly and improved reliability. Wave winding employs a zigzag pattern to optimize electromagnetic characteristics, enhancing torque and power density. Each type contributes uniquely to the performance and design flexibility of flatwire motors across various applications.</p></p>
