<p><strong>The IO-Link Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IO-Link Masters</li><li>IO-Link Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IO-Link Masters are devices that act as the central hub in IO-Link networks, connecting various IO-Link devices to the control system. They provide communication between sensors, actuators, and other automation components. IO-Link Devices include sensors, actuators, and other input/output devices that communicate digitally with the IO-Link Master. These devices offer various functionalities like diagnostics, parameterization, and real-time data transfer. The IO-Link Technology market consists of these two main segments, catering to the growing demand for flexible and intelligent industrial automation solutions.</p></p>
