<p><p>Cutting fluid is a vital component in the metalworking industry, with two main types: cooling and lubrication. Cooling cutting fluids are designed to dissipate heat generated during the cutting process, preventing overheating of the tools and workpiece. Lubrication cutting fluids, on the other hand, are used to reduce friction between the cutting tool and workpiece, promoting smoother and more efficient cutting operations. Both types play a crucial role in improving tool life, surface finish, and overall machining efficiency.</p></p>
