<p><strong>The Run-Flat Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting</li><li>Self-sealing</li><li>Auxiliary-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Run-flat tires come in three main types: self-supporting, self-sealing, and auxiliary-supported. Self-supporting tires have reinforced sidewalls that can support the weight of the vehicle even when air pressure is lost. Self-sealing tires have a special lining that can seal punctures automatically to prevent air loss. Auxiliary-supported tires use a support ring inside the tire to keep it stable in case of air loss. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different driving conditions and preferences.</p></p>
