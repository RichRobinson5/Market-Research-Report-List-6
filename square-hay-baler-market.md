<p><p>Square hay balers are available in two main types: variable chamber balers and fixed chamber balers. Both types cater to the needs of farmers and agricultural workers, with variable chamber balers offering flexibility and adaptability, while fixed chamber balers provide consistency and simplicity.</p></p>
