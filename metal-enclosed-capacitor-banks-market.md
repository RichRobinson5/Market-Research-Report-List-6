LV capacitor banks are used in residential and commercial applications, while MV capacitor banks are commonly used in industrial settings. HV capacitor banks are used in high voltage systems such as substations and power plants. Each type of capacitor bank serves a specific voltage range and application to improve power factor and reduce energy costs.</p></p>
