<p><p>Sickle-cell anemia therapeutics market includes blood transfusion, pharmacotherapy, and bone marrow transplant. Blood transfusion involves receiving healthy red blood cells to replace sickle-shaped cells. Pharmacotherapy uses medications to manage symptoms and complications of the disease, such as pain and infections. Bone marrow transplant is a potential cure for sickle-cell anemia, where healthy stem cells are transplanted to replace diseased cells. Each type of treatment plays a crucial role in improving the quality of life for patients with sickle-cell anemia.</p></p>
