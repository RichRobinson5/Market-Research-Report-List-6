<p><p>Nitrocellulose membrane filters are categorized based on pore sizes: below 0.45 µm, 0.45 to 6 µm, and above 6 µm. Filters with below 0.45 µm pore sizes are used for fine particle filtration, such as bacteria and viruses. Filters with 0.45 to 6 µm pore sizes are ideal for general filtration applications, while filters with above 6 µm pore sizes are suitable for larger particle filtration. Each type of filter caters to different filtration needs in various industries such as pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and research.</p></p>
