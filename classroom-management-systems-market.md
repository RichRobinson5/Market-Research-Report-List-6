<p><p>Classroom Management Systems can be categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems are installed and maintained on the school or institution's servers, offering more control and customization but requiring higher initial costs and IT resources. Cloud-Based systems, on the other hand, are hosted online, providing easier accessibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Schools can choose the type of system that best suits their needs based on factors such as budget, IT infrastructure, and desired level of control.</p></p>
