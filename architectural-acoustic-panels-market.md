<p><ul><li>Acoustic Membranes</li><li>Resonators Panel</li><li>Porous Material Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Architectural acoustic panels come in various types to suit different needs. Acoustic membranes panels are thin materials that absorb sound waves and reduce noise levels. Resonator panels use specially designed cavities to absorb and disperse sound waves effectively. Porous material panels utilize materials with porous surfaces to absorb and reduce sound reflections. Each of these types offers unique benefits for controlling sound within architectural spaces, making them popular choices in various settings such as offices, theaters, and recording studios.</p></p>
