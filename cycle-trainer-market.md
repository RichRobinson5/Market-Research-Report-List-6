<p><p>Cycle trainer market can be divided into two main types: Classic Trainers and Smart Trainers. Classic trainers are basic, non-interactive models that allow cyclists to pedal indoors. Smart trainers, on the other hand, have built-in technology that connects to cycling apps or programs, offering interactive virtual riding experiences. They automatically adjust resistance based on the chosen route or workout program, providing a more immersive and engaging training session for cyclists.</p></p>
