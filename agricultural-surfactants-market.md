<p><p>Agricultural surfactants market can be categorized into four types: Non-ionic, Anionic, Cationic, and Amphoteric. Non-ionic surfactants are versatile and work well in various water conditions. Anionic surfactants are mainly used for herbicides. Cationic surfactants are commonly used in fungicides and insecticides. Amphoteric surfactants have a balanced charge and are effective in a wide range of agricultural applications. Each type has specific characteristics that make them suitable for different purposes in agriculture.</p></p>
