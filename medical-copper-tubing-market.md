<p><p>Medical copper tubing market types include Type K Pipe and Type L Pipe. Type K Pipe is known for its thick walls, making it ideal for underground installations or harsh environments. It is commonly used for medical gas distribution systems in healthcare facilities. On the other hand, Type L Pipe has medium walls and is suitable for aboveground applications, such as water distribution in medical facilities. Both types of copper tubing are corrosion-resistant, durable, and provide excellent heat conductivity for medical applications.</p></p>
