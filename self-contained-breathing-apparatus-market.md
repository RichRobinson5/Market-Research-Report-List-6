<p><p>Self-contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) are used by firefighters, rescue workers, and industrial workers to provide breathable air in hazardous environments. There are two main types of SCBA on the market: Open-Circuit SCBA and Closed-Circuit SCBA. Open-Circuit SCBA release exhaled air into the atmosphere, while Closed-Circuit SCBA recycles exhaled air by removing carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen. Both types offer respiratory protection in different environments depending on the level of oxygen required and the duration of use.</p></p>
