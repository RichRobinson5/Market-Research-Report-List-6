<p><ul><li>Compressor Type Dehumidifiers</li><li>Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential portable dehumidifiers come in two main types: compressor type and desiccant type. Compressor type dehumidifiers work by drawing in air which is then cooled to condense the moisture, while desiccant type dehumidifiers absorb moisture through a desiccant material. Compressor type dehumidifiers are more common and effective in removing larger amounts of moisture in warmer climates, while desiccant type dehumidifiers are more suitable for cooler temperatures and lower humidity levels. Both types serve the purpose of reducing humidity levels in indoor spaces.</p></p>
