<p><ul><li>Analytical Ferrography</li><li>Direct-Reading Ferrography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ferrography testing market consists of two main types: analytical ferrography and direct-reading ferrography. Analytical ferrography involves analyzing wear particles collected from lubricants to identify potential issues in machinery. On the other hand, direct-reading ferrography provides immediate results by visually assessing wear particles under a microscope. Both methods are crucial in predicting and preventing mechanical failures, ensuring the smooth operation of machinery in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
