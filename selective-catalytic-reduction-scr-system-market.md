<p><p>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) System Market includes three main types: NH3-SCR, Urea-SCR, and Ammonia-SCR. NH3-SCR systems use ammonia gas as the reducing agent, while Urea-SCR systems use a urea-water solution. Ammonia-SCR systems directly use ammonia as the reducing agent. Each type of SCR system has its own advantages and limitations, but they all work by converting harmful nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water through a catalyst, reducing emissions from vehicles and industrial processes.</p></p>
