<p><p>Healthcare Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) market can be divided into two main types - Cloud-based PACS and On-premise PACS. </p><p>Cloud-based PACS allows healthcare providers to store, access, and share medical images and reports securely through cloud technology. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>On the other hand, On-premise PACS involves storing and managing medical images and reports on local servers within the healthcare facility. This type of system provides more control over data security and customization options but may require higher upfront investment and maintenance costs.</p></p>
