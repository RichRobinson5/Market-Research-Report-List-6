<p><p>Patient lateral transfer is the process of safely moving patients from one surface to another, such as from a bed to a stretcher or vice versa. This movement is crucial to prevent injuries to both patients and healthcare workers, as improper transfers can lead to musculoskeletal injuries for healthcare staff and pressure ulcers for patients. Various equipment and devices are used for patient lateral transfers, including slide sheets, transfer boards, air-assisted devices, and lateral transfer aids.</p><p>The patient lateral transfer market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as the increasing aging population, rising prevalence of chronic diseases, and growing awareness about the importance of safe patient handling. Technological advancements in patient transfer equipment and devices are also contributing to market growth, as manufacturers are developing products that are safer, easier to use, and more efficient.</p><p>The Patient Lateral Transfer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15.00% during the forecast period.
