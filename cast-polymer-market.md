<p><ul><li>Solid Surface</li><li>Cultured Marble</li><li>Engineered Stone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast polymer market consists of three main types: solid surface, cultured marble, and engineered stone. Solid surface materials are composed of acrylic, polyester, or a combination, offering a durable and seamless finish. Cultured marble mimics the look of natural marble but is more affordable and easier to maintain. Engineered stone, also known as quartz, is a blend of natural stone and resin, providing a high-end appearance with enhanced durability. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and budget considerations within the cast polymer market.</p></p>
