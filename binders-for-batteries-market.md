<p><p>Anode binder and cathode binder are types of binders used in the manufacturing of batteries. Anode binder helps to hold together the active materials in the anode section of the battery, while cathode binder serves the same purpose for the cathode section. These binders are crucial in ensuring the stability and performance of the battery, as they help to prevent the active materials from breaking apart or becoming detached during the charging and discharging processes. The market for anode and cathode binders is growing due to the increasing demand for high-performance batteries in various industries.</p></p>
