<p><p>PCB coverlays are used in various types of printed circuit boards (PCBs) including single-sided, double-sided, and multilayer PCBs. In single-sided PCBs, the coverlay is applied only on one side of the board, providing protection and insulation. In double-sided PCBs, the coverlay is applied on both sides to ensure enhanced durability and reliability. In multilayer PCBs, the coverlay is used to encapsulate and protect the inner conductive layers, allowing for complex and compact electronic designs.</p></p>
