<p><p>Dental air polishing units come in two main types: Table Top Polisher and Handy Polisher. Table Top Polishers are larger units that are typically stationary and used in dental offices for more heavy-duty polishing procedures. Handy Polishers, on the other hand, are smaller, portable units that are convenient for on-the-go or mobile dental practitioners. Both types of polishers use compressed air and water to deliver a controlled stream of polishing particles to remove plaque and stains from teeth.</p></p>
