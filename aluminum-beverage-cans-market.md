<p><p>The aluminum beverage cans market can be divided into two main types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by assembling three separate components - a top, a body, and a bottom. These cans are typically used for packaging non-carbonated beverages. On the other hand, two-piece cans are made from a single piece of aluminum and are commonly used for carbonated drinks due to their ability to withstand high internal pressure. Both types of cans are widely used in the beverage industry.</p></p>
