PH meters are used to measure the acidity or alkalinity of water. Dissolved oxygen analyzers measure the amount of oxygen present in water. Conductivity sensors measure the water's ability to conduct electrical currents. Turbidity meters measure the clarity of water by analyzing suspended particles. Other equipment includes devices for monitoring parameters such as temperature, salinity, and chlorine levels.</p></p>
