Corn Gluten, on the other hand, is a byproduct of corn processing and is often used as a valuable source of protein in animal feed. Other Gluten refers to gluten extracted from alternative sources such as barley or rye. These different types cater to various industries and dietary preferences, offering a versatile range of applications.</p></p>
