<p><p>Electromagnetic fail safe brakes are classified into three different types based on their operation mechanism. Spring Applied Brakes activate when power is cut off, using springs to engage the brake. Permanent Magnet Brakes rely on a permanent magnet to engage the brake when power is removed. Permanent Magnet Toothed Brakes feature toothed rotors that engage with the magnetic field to create braking force when power is lost. Each type of brake offers unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
