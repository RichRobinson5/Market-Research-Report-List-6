<p><p>Two way cartridge valve market encompasses various types based on their specific functions. Intercepting valves are used to shut off flow in one direction, while back-pressure valves maintain pressure in a system. Safety valves release excess pressure to prevent damage. Regulating valves control flow rate, splite-flow valves divide flow into multiple paths, and others provide custom functions. Each type serves a unique purpose in ensuring efficient and safe operation of hydraulic systems in various industries.</p></p>
