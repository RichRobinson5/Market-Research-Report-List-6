<p><p>Aluminium Composite Panels (ACP) are flat panels composed of a thermoplastic core sandwiched between two aluminium sheets. They are widely used in building construction due to their lightweight, durability, and corrosion resistance. ACPs are popular for applications such as external cladding, façade systems, signage, and interior decoration.</p><p>The Aluminium Composite Panels Market is expected to witness significant growth, with a CAGR of 6.70% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for sustainable and energy-efficient building materials, along with the growing construction industry worldwide, is driving the market growth. The rise in urbanization and infrastructural development projects in emerging economies is also bolstering the demand for ACPs.</p><p>Key trends in the Aluminium Composite Panels Market include the development of innovative products with enhanced fire retardant properties, improved thermal insulation, and customizable designs to meet the specific requirements of architects and builders. Overall, the market for Aluminium Composite Panels is poised for steady growth in the coming years.</p></p>
