<p><p>Raised access floor systems are commonly categorized into four main types: steel encapsulated, calcium sulphate board, aluminum board, and chipboard encapsulated. Steel encapsulated access floors feature a steel shell that provides durability and strength. Calcium sulphate board access floors are known for their high load-bearing capacity and fire resistance. Aluminum board access floors are lightweight and corrosion-resistant. Chipboard encapsulated access floors are cost-effective, offering good strength and durability. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
