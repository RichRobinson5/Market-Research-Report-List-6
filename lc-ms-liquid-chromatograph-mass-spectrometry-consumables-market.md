<p><p>LC-MS consumables market consists of various types such as Ion Source, Chromatographic Column, Filter, Injector, Nitrogen Generator, and Others. Ion Source is used to ionize the sample, while Chromatographic Column separates the sample components. Filters are utilized for sample purification, and Injectors introduce the sample into the system. Nitrogen Generators supply nitrogen gas for the instrument. Other consumables may include syringes, vials, and fittings essential for the operation of LC-MS systems. Each type plays a critical role in the performance and efficiency of the instrument.</p></p>
