<p><p>The Rubber Masterbatch market consists of various types based on the rubber source. EPDM-based masterbatch offers excellent weather resistance and is ideal for outdoor applications. SBR-based masterbatch, known for its good abrasion resistance, is widely used in tires and automotive parts. NBR-based masterbatch provides excellent oil and chemical resistance, making it suitable for industrial applications. Natural rubber-based masterbatch is valued for its elasticity and strength, commonly used in consumer goods and various rubber products. Each type serves specific application needs.</p></p>
