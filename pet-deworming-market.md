<p><p>Internal deworming refers to the market for products that treat worms and parasites inside the pet's body, typically administered orally. This includes medications for roundworms, tapeworms, and hookworms. External deworming, on the other hand, involves products that treat external parasites like fleas, ticks, and mites. These products are usually applied topically or through collars. Both internal and external deworming are essential to maintaining a pet's health and preventing the spread of parasites to other animals or humans.</p></p>
