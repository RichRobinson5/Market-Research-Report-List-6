<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering System (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li><li>DP-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering System (EPS) market has several types based on the mechanism used to assist steering. Column-EPS (C-EPS) uses an electric motor connected to the steering column, while Pinion-EPS (P-EPS) has the motor directly mounted on the steering rack. Rack-EPS (R-EPS) uses a separate motor and rack assembly, while Dual Pinion-EPS (DP-EPS) has two motors for improved response and performance. These different types cater to various vehicle models and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
