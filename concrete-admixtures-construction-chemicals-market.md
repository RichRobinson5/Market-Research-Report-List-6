<p><p>Concrete admixtures are construction chemicals used to enhance the properties of concrete. Mineral admixtures such as fly ash, silica fume, and slag are added to improve workability, strength, and durability of concrete. Chemical admixtures, on the other hand, include superplasticizers, accelerators, retarders, and water reducers that modify the properties of concrete, such as setting time, flowability, and strength. Both mineral and chemical admixtures play a crucial role in the construction industry, offering a wide range of benefits for various applications.</p></p>
